How to Play Windows Games on Linux

You can play most Windows-only games from GameJolt directly on Linux! Our community uses Steam's **Proton** compatibility tool for this.

Here’s the quick method:

  • Add the game's executable file (.exe) as a "Non-Steam Game" to your Steam Library.
  • In the game's **Properties**, go to the **Compatibility** tab and check the box to "Force the use of a specific Steam Play compatibility tool" (select a **Proton** version).
